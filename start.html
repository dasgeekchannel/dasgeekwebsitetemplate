DasGeek Communities

This is a community of geeks with a common goal of filling our brains. This community is meant for sharing and exploring new ideas and passions with fellow geeks of all kinds from around the world. Since ignorance has no place in a community of brain expanding geniuses we accept 'all' people without exception. This is a place of peace of learning. Not a safe place where your ideals won't be challenged but rather a place where you can teach and be taught. Make friends, be kind, be thoughtful and add to the conversations. Name calling and trolling is great for 4chan or Reddit but will not be tolerated here. Here we learn. Here we teach. Here we grow our brains. Welcome to the geeks only club.

Must Bookmark Sites

  • AlternativeTo
    • Offers alternative opensource software options for most programs on the market. Looking to replace Adobe Premiere or OneNote, use this site and enjoy the rated alternatives.
  • Code Academy
    • Great site to learn how to program for free. Easy to use tutorials and live labs that let you put your newly learned coding into action and see the results.
  • DuckDuckGo
    • My absolute favorite search engine. Results as good as Google and it doesn't track you and follow you around with ads. Reliable results with fantastic speed and privacy. All real geeks use DuckDuckGo.
  • LifeHacker
    • My absolute favorite tech site for getting great tips, tricks, and trusted reviews.
